About Me

I’m Roue Cuadra, an IMD student who loves everything aesthetic. I’m an outgoing introvert who believes that unhappiness isn’t always a weakness. I’m a true-blue beach bum, and I dream of retiring and living in a tree house by the beach one day.

When I’m not tinkering with codes or design, you’ll find me daydreaming about travel, food and Japanese anime. I enjoy snorkelling and I’m interested to learn free diving. Alternative, rock and grunge music keeps me sane, and I used to fight my way out of mosh pits during concerts.
